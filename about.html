Who We Are

Established in 2023, Abacus aims to provide math education to kids at an early age (Grades 1-3). Unlike other tutorial centers, we don't stop at teaching basic arithmetic; our lessons give a solid foundation for kids to learn about higher-level math such as algebra in later years. We believe that by introducing kids to these concepts at a young age, while their brains are rapidly growing, they will gain a head start in learning math not just now, but even in the future. Here at Abacus, we believe that it's never too early to start being a Math genius!

What We Provide

Here are three reasons why you should choose Abacus for your child's education:

High-Quality Lessons

Our lessons are written with the help of math teachers and professionals to ensure they are accurate and up-to-date.

Online Activities

Our website contains many fun activities to help children understand math concepts in an engaging way.


Helpful Resources

Our website has various supplemental materials that parents can use to assist their child in challenging lessons.
